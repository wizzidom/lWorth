The Elginist Group, our approach to sustainability aims to enable the business to meet the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. Our focus is on creating a sustainable company, communities and planet on which consumers are enabled to

improve their health and well-being, livelihoods are improved, and we significantly reduce our environmental footprint. sustainability strategy articulates our strategic Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) priorities. The strategy includes commitments across three clear focus areas: health and

nutrition, enhanced livelihoods, and environmental stewardship. These are the areas where Tiger Brands, as Africa’s largest food company, believes it can harness its core activities most effectively, to create a positive ESG impact.
